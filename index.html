About Ashless

Ashless is your personal quit smoking tracker designed to help you monitor your progress on your journey to becoming smoke-free.

How it works:

  • Track your daily cravings and cigarettes smoked
  • Monitor money spent (custom price per cigarette)
  • Add notes to track triggers and feelings
  • Visualize your progress with charts

Tips for Success:

  • Log every craving and cigarette
  • Review your progress weekly
  • Use notes to identify patterns
  • Celebrate small victories!

"Every cigarette you don't smoke is a victory. Keep going!"
